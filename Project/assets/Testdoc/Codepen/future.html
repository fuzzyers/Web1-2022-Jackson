We're proud to say that we only source Fairtrade certified coffee beans, cocoa and cane sugar.

Through our partnership with the Fair Climate Fund, we offset the carbon on every kilogram of coffee we roast.

All of our coffee and drinking chocolate is certified organic through BioGro New Zealand. That's a whole lot of organic goodness.

You might call them dirtbags, but we love that our coffee and drinking chocolate packaging is all compostable.
